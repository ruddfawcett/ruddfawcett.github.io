#Notepad

A simple Markdown editor.

Notepad gives your users the ability to **write and edit Markdown** directly in your app.

Oh, and did I mention that, with a JSON stylesheet, it's *fully themeable*?

Add it to your app with only `a few lines of code`.

Notepad is fully hackable and open source. Be sure to [check it out on GitHub](https://github.com/ruddfawcett/Notepad).

Enjoy.
